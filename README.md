# Product Hunt UX Prospector

Automate your SaaS prospecting workflow from Product Hunt to LinkedIn founder outreach with premium UX insights.

## üöÄ Overview
Product Hunt UX Prospector is a powerful Chrome extension designed for founders, marketers, and researchers to quickly evaluate new SaaS launches. It bridges the gap between discovering a product on Product Hunt and initiating a meaningful outreach on LinkedIn.

## ‚ú® Key Features
- **One-Click Prospecting:** Automatically opens the top 15 products of the day from Product Hunt in new tabs.
- **On-Page Evaluation Panel:** Inject a floating UI into any SaaS homepage to evaluate H1 headlines, CTAs, and navigation instantly.
- **UX Scoring:** Rate the clarity and flow of a landing page on a 1-5 scale.
- **Smart Data Extraction:** Automatically pulls social links, headers, and meta descriptions.
- **Shortlist Manager:** Keep track of your best prospects and export them as high-quality PDF or JSON for your CRM.
- **LinkedIn Founder Search:** Jump directly to a LinkedIn search for the company's founder with one click.
- **AI-Ready DM Generation:** Copies a professional outreach template based on the page's actual data to your clipboard.

## üõ†Ô∏è Installation (Developer Mode)
1. Clone this repository or download the source code.
2. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Enable **Developer mode** in the top-right corner.
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the folder containing this extension.
5. The extension is now ready to use!

## üìñ How to Use
1. **Navigate to Product Hunt:** Go to the "Today" or "Yesterday" feed.
2. **Launch Prospector:** Click the extension icon and hit **"Start Prospecting"**.
3. **Evaluate:** As tabs open, use the floating side panel to rate and shortlist promising products.
4. **Outreach:** Use the "Find Founder" and "Generate DM" buttons to start your connection.
5. **Manage:** Open the extension popup anytime to view your shortlist and export your data.
